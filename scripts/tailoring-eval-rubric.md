# Tailoring Eval Rubric

## Goal
Evaluate whether AI resume tailoring is professional, truthful, ATS-aware, and aligned to the target role.

## Dimensions

1. Headline Accuracy (0-2)
- 2: Matches the JD title exactly or nearly exactly without invented seniority.
- 1: Mostly aligned but paraphrased or slightly off.
- 0: Generic or misleading.

2. Summary Fit (0-2)
- 2: Mirrors the role's actual needs and foregrounds the strongest relevant evidence.
- 1: Reasonable but generic.
- 0: Weakly relevant or padded.

3. Evidence Fidelity (0-2)
- 2: No invented tools, ownership, metrics, or domain claims.
- 1: Slight embellishment risk.
- 0: Clear fabrication or overclaiming.

4. Skills Alignment (0-2)
- 2: Prioritizes JD-relevant keywords and preserves believable categories.
- 1: Partially aligned.
- 0: Mostly generic or noisy.

5. Experience Rewrite Quality (0-2)
- 2: Bullets are clearer, more targeted, and still truthful.
- 1: Some improvement but uneven.
- 0: No useful rewrite or obvious distortion.

6. Layout / Emphasis Logic (0-2)
- 2: Section order and omissions support the application strategy.
- 1: Neutral.
- 0: Hurts readability or focus.

## Overall Interpretation
- 10-12: Strong, professional tailoring
- 7-9: Usable but should be reviewed
- 4-6: Weak or too generic
- 0-3: Unsafe or low-quality
